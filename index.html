About Me

Hello! I'm Loice, an engineering leader with a passion for building impactful technology and empowering high-performing teams. My journey spans from hands-on software engineering at Microsoft and AWS to leading engineering teams and co-founding companies.

At AWS, I enhanced security and compliance for billing services, redesigned authentication processes, and developed auditor tools that significantly improved billing accuracy and revenue collection across multiple accounts. Before that, at Microsoft, I worked on Azure Host OS servicing, ensuring 99.9% uptime for Azure services, and contributed to Windows Admin Center, including implementing accessibility features for differently-abled users.

Currently, as Co-Founder & Managing Director at Acoruss and Head of Engineering at Gapstack, I combine technical expertise with strategic leadership to drive innovation. I've also dedicated years to mentoring developers at Andela, organizing knowledge-sharing sessions and strengthening client relationships through effective collaboration.

My philosophy is simple: Team first, learning second, then delivery. This approach has helped me build cohesive, high-performing teams that consistently exceed expectations.
